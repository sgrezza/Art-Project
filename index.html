Project 2

The Room Project

Kickstart my art

This project's purpose was to design the walls of an exhibition.

When you walk into the room, this wall would be on your left. The white shape on the bottom is a button that, when pressed, illuminates the word "Functional" along with the light bars on either side of the switch.


This is the floor of the room. It exhibits the path intented for the visitor to walk, and the path the visitor will likely take.


This is the wall in front of you when you walk in. It exhibits what happens when something is "over-designed", which make the user feel over-stimulated and saturated with unneccesarry information.


This is the wall behind you, the grey square on the bottom is the entrance. The intention is to show that any message should be given in a clear way, not a cool way.


This is the wall to your right when you walk in. The intent of this wall is to show the simplicity of something as mundane as an exit sign - no arrows, no flashing lights, just a static, boring word.


This project was my second favorite project, besides the book project. Working with type is something I enjoy, and that's all this project was. The fonts used were Justus Pro, San Fransisco Display, and Roboto.
